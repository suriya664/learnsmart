Our Story

Founded in 2018 in Bengaluru

LearnSmart Academy was founded by a team of IIT and IIM alumni who believed that quality education should be accessible to everyone, regardless of their location or background.

What started as a small initiative with just 10 courses has now grown into India's leading online education platform with over 5,000 courses, 800+ expert instructors, and 150,000+ happy students.

Today, we're proud to have helped thousands of students across India build successful careers in technology, business, and creative fields.

Our Mission

To democratize education in India by providing affordable, high-quality online courses that help students and professionals achieve their career goals and transform their lives.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted online education platform in India, empowering 1 million+ students by 2030 with skills that matter in the digital economy.
